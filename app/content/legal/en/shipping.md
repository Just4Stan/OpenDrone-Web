## Shipping and delivery

Incutec BV ships orders from Belgium to most European Union countries. Delivery times and shipping costs are calculated and displayed at checkout before payment.

The Dutch version (/shipping) is the legally authoritative text for consumers residing in Belgium. This English translation is informative only.

### Delivery time

Orders are normally shipped within 2 business days of payment receipt. The final delivery deadline is 30 calendar days after the order, unless explicitly agreed otherwise (Art. VI.43 of the Belgian Code of Economic Law, Art. 7 of our General Terms and Conditions). If the final delivery deadline is not met, the consumer has the right to rescind the contract free of charge.

### Shipping rates and countries

Shipping costs depend on the destination and parcel weight. Some countries are excluded due to export sanctions — see our [export compliance page](/export-compliance).

### Risk during shipping

The risk of damage or loss in transit lies with Incutec BV until the moment of delivery to the consumer (Art. VI.44 of the Belgian Code of Economic Law).

### Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email confirmation with a tracking number.
